## Funny Birds: Revenge
Funny Birds: Revenge is an arcade game where you control the bird with your finger, collect bonuses and fight enemies!

## 🎮 Gameplay
- 👆 **Control**: Lead the bird with your finger - it will follow you
- 🎯 **Goal**: Score as many points as possible
- ⚡ **Bonuses**: Collect flies and strawberries
- 👾 **Enemies**: Avoid a bird encounter! And at some point, the boss and special birds will appear
